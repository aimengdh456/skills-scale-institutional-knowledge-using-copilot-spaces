# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## QA Lead / Test Engineer

### Role Summary
QA Leads and Test Engineers ensure product quality through testing strategy, test automation, and validation of acceptance criteria. They work closely with developers, product managers, and designers to identify quality risks early and maintain high standards throughout the delivery lifecycle.

### Responsibilities
- Define test strategy and test plans for features and releases
- Design, implement, and maintain automated test suites
- Perform manual testing for complex scenarios and user flows
- Validate acceptance criteria and definition of done
- Identify, document, and track defects through resolution
- Collaborate with developers on testability and quality metrics
- Report on quality metrics and test coverage

### Goals
- Ensure features meet quality standards before release
- Increase test automation coverage and reduce manual testing burden
- Catch defects early in the development cycle
- Maintain fast, reliable CI/CD pipelines

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups to report testing status and blockers
- Test plan reviews with developers and product managers
- Bug triage meetings to prioritize defects
- Release readiness reviews

### Collaboration Points
- Works with developers during design to ensure testability
- Partners with product managers to clarify acceptance criteria
- Coordinates with UX/UI designers to validate user flows
- Aligns with release managers on deployment verification

---

## UX/UI Designer

### Role Summary
UX/UI Designers create user-centered designs that balance usability, accessibility, and business goals. They research user needs, design interfaces and interactions, and validate designs through testing and feedback.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, mockups, and interactive prototypes
- Design user interfaces following design systems and accessibility standards
- Collaborate with product managers to translate requirements into designs
- Work with developers to ensure design implementation fidelity
- Validate released features match design intent and user needs

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible user experiences
- Reduce user friction and improve task completion rates
- Maintain design consistency across the product
- Advocate for user needs in product decisions

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews with product and engineering teams
- User research findings and insights presentations
- Collaboration sessions with developers during implementation
- Design system updates and documentation

### Collaboration Points
- Partners with product managers on feature definition and user stories
- Works with developers during planning to assess technical feasibility
- Collaborates with QA on usability testing and acceptance criteria
- Provides design assets and specifications for implementation

---

## Tech Lead / Solution Architect

### Role Summary
Tech Leads and Solution Architects provide technical leadership, architectural guidance, and mentorship to development teams. They ensure technical decisions align with long-term goals, scalability requirements, and best practices.

### Responsibilities
- Define technical architecture and design patterns
- Make technology stack and tooling decisions
- Review complex technical designs and code
- Mentor developers on best practices and coding standards
- Identify technical debt and propose refactoring strategies
- Ensure security, performance, and scalability requirements are met
- Bridge technical and business stakeholders

### Goals
- Deliver scalable, maintainable technical solutions
- Reduce technical debt and improve code quality
- Build team technical capabilities through mentorship
- Align technical decisions with business objectives

### Typical Communication
- Architecture review meetings and technical design discussions
- Weekly technical planning with project and product managers
- Code reviews and pair programming sessions
- Technical documentation and decision records

### Collaboration Points
- Works with product managers to translate business requirements into technical solutions
- Partners with project managers on estimating and planning complex work
- Guides developers on implementation approaches and standards
- Collaborates with QA on test strategy and quality metrics

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts bridge business needs and technical solutions by gathering requirements, analyzing processes, and ensuring delivered features meet stakeholder expectations. They facilitate communication between business stakeholders and delivery teams.

### Responsibilities
- Gather and document business requirements
- Analyze current processes and identify improvement opportunities
- Create detailed user stories and acceptance criteria
- Facilitate requirements workshops with stakeholders
- Validate solutions against business needs
- Support user acceptance testing (UAT)
- Document business processes and workflows

### Goals
- Ensure delivered solutions meet business objectives
- Reduce rework by clarifying requirements upfront
- Improve stakeholder satisfaction and adoption
- Enable data-driven decision making

### Typical Communication
- Requirements gathering sessions with stakeholders
- Backlog refinement meetings with product and project teams
- UAT coordination and results reporting
- Process documentation and training materials

### Collaboration Points
- Partners with product managers to translate business needs into product requirements
- Works with designers to ensure business workflows are reflected in UX
- Collaborates with developers to clarify requirements and edge cases
- Coordinates with support teams on documentation and training

---

## Support / Customer Success Manager

### Role Summary
Support and Customer Success Managers ensure customers successfully adopt and derive value from OctoAcme products. They handle customer inquiries, escalate issues, gather feedback, and advocate for customer needs in product development.

### Responsibilities
- Respond to customer questions and technical issues
- Triage and escalate bugs to engineering teams
- Document known issues, workarounds, and solutions
- Gather and synthesize customer feedback for product teams
- Create and maintain customer-facing documentation
- Track customer health metrics and satisfaction scores
- Conduct customer onboarding and training

### Goals
- Maximize customer satisfaction and retention
- Minimize time-to-resolution for customer issues
- Provide actionable product feedback to improve user experience
- Enable customer self-service through documentation

### Typical Communication
- Daily issue triage and escalation to engineering
- Weekly customer health reviews with product and leadership
- Customer feedback sessions and feature request prioritization
- Release communications and customer education

### Collaboration Points
- Escalates bugs and feature requests to product and engineering teams
- Partners with QA to validate fixes before communicating to customers
- Works with business analysts and product managers on requirements and feedback
- Collaborates with designers on documentation and training materials
